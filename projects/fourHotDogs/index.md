# Four Hotdogs

## Preparation
- Press grapes
- Oven 90°
- Chop bacon
- Bacon with water
- Lots of onions for three separate into (a) ready, (b) wine sauce, and (c) pisto
- Mushroom
- Pisto: onion, pepper, carrot, garlic, tomatoes, zucchini, herbs
- Mushroom sauce: Onion, mushroom, black garlic, reduced wine, beef bouillon
- Warm up sauerkraut in the oven
- Grind old cheese
- Press chips
- Grind cashews 
- Blend mushroom sauce
- Everything in the oven

## Hotdogs
- Boil the sausages
- Open buns
- Add buns to oven
- Fry two sausages

### Mosel valley
- Sauerkraut
- Mustard

### Alsace
- Onion set aside
- Creme fraise
- Bacon

### Provence
- Pisto on the lower side
- Chips

### Piedmont
- Mushroom sauce on the lower side
- Truffle pesto
- Cashews
