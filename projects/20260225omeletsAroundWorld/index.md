# Omelets around the world

Chop ginger (x2)
Sauté the ginger in two pans, one with butter and the other with neutral oil
Chop onion (x4)
When ginger is done, set aside and clean the skillets
Sauté onion (x4)  in two pans (2x2) with butter and with olive oil
Chop celery (x3)
Set aside half the olive oil onion
Add celery to the butter onion (x2) and to the olive onion (x1)
Chop carrot (x2)
Set aside half the butter onion+celery
Add the carrot to the butter onion+celery (x1) * and to the olive onion (x1) * 
Chop bell peppers x1 for red and x1 for green, 
Set aside both skillets and clean them
Chop garlic (x5) and tomato (x2)
Saute red pepper in olive oil
Saute green pepper in butter
When red pepper is ready, add garlic (x1), tomato (x1), and cooked olive oil onion (x1) *
Clean this skillet
When green pepper is ready, add garlic (x1), and the set aside butter onion+celery (x1) *
Clean this skillet
Add butter again, add garlic and the cooked ginger (x1) *
Clean this skillet.
Neutral oil, add garlic (x2), green onion (x2), split into two, adding tomato (x1) * to one and the cooked ginger (x1) to the other *
