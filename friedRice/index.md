# Fried Rice

This dish is based the recepie of Elizabeth Haigh.

## Ingredients
For the rice:
- Chicken stock
- Garlic
- Pandan leaves
- Ginger
- Chicken fat

For the dish
- Garlic
- Shallot
- Spring onions
- Chilly
- Eggs
- Soy sauce
- MSG
- Sesame oil
- High heat oil
- White pepper

## Instructions

### The day before
Make a *simple* version of **Hainanese** rice with
- chicken stock,
- garlic,
- pandan leaves,
- ginger, and
- chicken fat.

### Preparation
- Chop:
  - Garlic
  - Shallot
  - Spring onions
  - Chilli pepper
- Whisk two eggs
- Prepare the rest of the ingredients
  -  Soy sauce
  -  MSG
  -  Sesame oil
  -  High heat oil
  -  White pepper

### The Cooking
- Wok on high heat
- PUt high heat oil in the wok
- In order, add:
  1. Shallot
  2. Garlic
  3. Egg
  4. Rice
- Once it is almost ready, add
  1. Soy sauce
  2. MSG
  3. White peper
  4. Chilli pepper
  5. Spring onion
  6. Sesame oil
- Plate
