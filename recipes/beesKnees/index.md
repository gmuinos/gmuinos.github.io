# The Bee's Knees

Trying something $b+a$ different $\frac{q}{f}$ and see what happens.

## Ingredients
- $2$ parts gin
- $1$ part lemon juice
- $\frac{1}{2}$ part honey syrup

## Steps
 - Honey syrup:
   - Combine equal parts of warm water and honey.
 - Cocktail:
   - Mix the gin, the lemon juice, and the honey syrup.
 - Serve with ice.
