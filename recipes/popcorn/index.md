# Pop Corn

## Ingredients
- Pop corn
- Salt
- Some fat[^1]

## Steps
- In a pot, add a layer of oil and two corns.
> The thickness of the layer should be a bit less than the heigh of the layed-down corns.
- Heat the pot at medium or medium-high heat.
- Once both corns have popped, set them aside and turn the heat off.
- Add the corns to the pot until it fills the bottom of the pot.
- Let them stay with the heat off for around two minutes.
- Heat the pot again to a medium-high level.
- When the corns start popping, crack the lid open.
- As soon as the popping stops for around two seconds, place all the pop corn inmediately on a bowl.

[^1]: Neutral oil is better if in doubt. This recipe is too warm for olive oil. Butter instead of oil takes the profile of butter.
